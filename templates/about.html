How it works:

Buying

sign in and click the big buy button
that's the currently lowest price!
once you buy, you'll be connected with the selling to then meet up and exchange money and food!!

Sellling

sign in and enter the minimum price you are willing to sell your guest meal for
you won't receive anything less than that price!
once someone purchases your meal, you will be notified via email and you then set up a time with the buyer to exchange food and money!
